ABOUT US

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Eco-Friendly

Committed to sustainable delivery solutions

Amidst the hustle and bustle of the modern world, we are increasingly concerned about the challenges of climate change and air pollution. In Vietnam, where our hearts are filled with love, KONA deeply understands those concerns.

Not only stopping at delivering delicious meals and multi-service, we aspire to contribute a small part to the journey of creating a greener Vietnam. With all enthusiasm and seriousness, KONA builds creative, flexible delivery solutions for a sustainable future for ourselves and future generations.

We don't just deliver, we deliver hope.

With a team full of enthusiasm, and a good vision to work together towards a single direction. We confidently guarantee that we may be late, but our projects and products are carefully built and of the quality that makes you satisfied.
